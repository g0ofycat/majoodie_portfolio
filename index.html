About Me

I'm a 16-year-old Roblox architect specializing in custom builds in Roblox Studio since 2018, with a professional focus since 2021. I’ve completed around 120 commissions for various studios and brands, including collaborations with Exclusible S.A (Louboutin) and Yama Studio (Camden Grave). I'm experienced in creating unique environments for Roblox-based advertising and am open to new projects. If you're interested in collaborating, reach out to create something exceptional together!
